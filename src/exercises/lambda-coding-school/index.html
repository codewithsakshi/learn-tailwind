Our ISA is an investment in you

Most schools make money regardless of whether or not their students succeed. Lambda School takes a different approach. We'll cover the cost of your upfront tuition and you'll pay us back 17% of your income for two years once you're earning an annual income of $50,000 or more.‍ The Binary School Income Share Agreement at a glance:

Binary School covers your tuition until you're making at least $50,000 a year. There's no down payment.

Once you’re hired, you’ll start making monthly payments based on your income. If your annual income dips below $50,000 for any reason, payments are automatically paused.

Payments end once you’ve reached the $30,000 cap or your payment schedule ends – whichever comes first.
